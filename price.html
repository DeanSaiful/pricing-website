    Common Problems

    Q1. Can I change my plan at any time?

    Yes. For now, everyone can enjoy all the features for free. You can also choose the corresponding paid version.

    Q2. Can I save my project locally?

    Yes, you can download your project locally or export it to Altium. Using our brand new desktop client-side, allowing you save all your projects locally instead of saving on the cloud server.

    Q3. EasyEDA tools are quite expensive to build, how can you supply the core features for free?

    Yes, it has taken more than 8 years to build EasyEDA to this point and we will continue developing it. We will be unique, making money from our services such as Part orders, PCB orders, PCB assembly, advertising and special project reviews but even so, we always appreciate it if you would like to make a donation or change to our professional plan :)
